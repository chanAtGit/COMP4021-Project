Player Instructions

Game Rules

The game consists of 3 rounds and each lasting up to 70 seconds. A player wins a round by depleting their opponent's HP. If the round ends with neither player dying, the player with a higher HP wins. A player wins the game by winning 2 out of 3 rounds.


Weapons & Potions

Random weapons and potions appear at fixed points in the arena for the players to collect. Only a maximum of 5 uncollected items are allowed on the arena and will be refreshed every 10 seconds if they are collected.

The weapons include SMG, Shotgun, and AR.

  • The SMG has a fast firing rate, but short range and low damage per bullet.
  • The Shotgun provides high burst damage at the cost of short range and slow firing rate.
  • The AR has the longest range out of the three weapons, as well as a medium firing rate and damage.

The game features no reload mechanism. Thus, when a player collects a weapon, they are limited by said weapon’s ammo.

The potions aid the players in combat.

  • The Defense potion increases the player's resistance against damage at the cost of their speed.
  • The Speed potion increases the player’s speed but decreases their damage resistance.
  • The Rage potion increases the player’s damage at the cost of their damage resistance.

Each potion takes 2.5 seconds to consume, and its effect lasts 8 seconds.


User Controls

WASD keys control the players' movements, and their avatar faces towards their cursor. 'Q' and 'E' are used to select the player's weapon and item slot, respectively. Holding the mouse's left key fires the player's weapon or consumes their potion. The 'F' key is used to collect items.


Sudden Death

The game will enter a sudden-death mode if a round reaches its last 15 seconds. By then, all obstacles will disappear, and the players will deal double the normal damage.
